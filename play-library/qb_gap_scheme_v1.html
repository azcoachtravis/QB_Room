THE QB IS ALWAYS ON THE FIELD
Gap scheme is downhill — but the QB is not a spectator. Every handoff has a fake. Every fake is a block. Carry out your action with conviction. Spectators sit in the stands. Never hand off and stop. Never hand off and watch. Run your fake every single time.
Gap Scheme — No Read
Hand It Off — Trust the Pull
Gap scheme is hat for hat by design. The pulling lineman is your extra blocker — he creates the advantage. Your job is to get the ball to the RB quickly and on track, then carry out your fake with purpose.
★ No reads. No hesitation. The scheme creates the edge — trust it.
Pulling Lineman = The Key
He Creates — You Deliver
The pull is the play. The pulling lineman kicks out the EMLOS or leads through the hole — everything happens off his path. Mesh the ball quickly, stay on track, and let the puller do his job.
★ Get the ball handed off clean — the pull takes care of the math.
⚡
Quick Mesh
Gap scheme lives and dies on the exchange. Get the ball to the RB fast — no lingering in the mesh.
📍
Ride the Track
Stay on your path through the handoff. Don't drift. The RB's aiming point is fixed — your track sets his.
🎭
Sell the Fake
Your fake after the handoff holds a backside defender. One hesitation by one defender is a block — make it count.
⚓
Stay Downhill
Don't drift back into the pocket after the give. Stay flat and aggressive through the mesh. Depth kills the scheme.

🔵 Toss — True Sweep · QB Pitches on Run · F Leads
Todd
Toss Sweep
Concept
True toss sweep — RB catches a pitch wide, F leads the edge
RB Aim
Get width fast, press the corner off the F's block — get vertical when the edge opens
QB Feet
Open to the toss side → gain depth → deliver a clean flat pitch to the RB → continue flat and sell a naked threat
Key Block
F leads and kicks the EMLOS or seals the edge — OL works to seal the second level
▶
Pitch must be clean and catchable — no bad pitches, no turnovers
▶
After pitch → continue flat, threaten the edge as a run threat
⚠
Pitch is the most dangerous exchange — get depth, get clean, deliver early
🔑 Toss Pitch Mechanics
Pitch Path
Open hips to the toss side and get depth — 2 steps back to create the proper pitch angle. Never pitch flat from the line — depth gives you a clean window and protects against a bad exchange.
Pitch Timing
Deliver the pitch early — before you feel pressure from the EMLOS. A late pitch is a fumble waiting to happen. Get the ball out of your hands and into the RB's before the edge collapses.

⚡ The Pull is the Play
Gap scheme creates the numbers advantage through the pull — not through reading a defender. Trust the design. Your job is not to make a decision — it is to execute the exchange cleanly and on time. A clean fast handoff on the right track lets the puller do his job. Don't think. Don't hesitate. Deliver the ball and run your fake.
🎭 Counter = Misdirection — Sell It
Connor and Cody live and die on your pre-snap and post-snap movement. Your body going playside is what freezes the backside defenders. If you don't sell the flow, the counter has nowhere to go. Move with conviction playside before and after the mesh. The best block you throw is your own body moving the wrong way.
