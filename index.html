Foundation
Core Principles
01
Numbers Tell You Who
The box count determines the key defender before the snap. Always start here.
02
Leverage Tells You Where
The corner's foot and hip shows where space is opening. Read the defender, not the receiver.
03
Pressure Overrides Both
Pressure detected = hot route at 1.8s. Key hierarchy suspended. No hesitation, ever.
04
Motion Tells the Truth
Man follows. Zone rotates. Motion confirms what SOCKS predicted.
05
Situation Defines the Gift
Down, clock, field position, hash. Situational awareness tells you what the defense is giving before they give it.
06
Mastery, Not Time
Stages are earned by performance. A freshman who masters Stage 1 advances immediately.
07
Never Skip a Stage
You cannot read leverage if you can't trust the count. The foundation never stops mattering.
★
TWTDGY
Take What The Defense Gives You. SOCKS tells you the gift. Motion confirms. Situation defines. Timing executes.
